ABOUT US

This software system will be an Online Job Portal System. This system will be designed as a web application built in java. It provides the candidates, ability to register to this application, Search for jobs and manage their accounts. Each candidate will have an account with their own profile page. It provides access of the projects uploaded by respective companies to the interested job seekers from anywhere. Employers those are willing to publish the jobs for the company to candidates, can register to this job portal and get their own account created and can also post jobs to portals database.
